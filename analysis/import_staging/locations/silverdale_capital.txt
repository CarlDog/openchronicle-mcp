Silverdale - The Shining Capital

Silverdale stands as the crown jewel of the kingdom, built upon seven hills overlooking the confluence of two major rivers. The city's name comes from the distinctive silver-veined marble used in its construction, which catches and reflects sunlight throughout the day, making the city visible from miles away.

The city is divided into distinct districts:

The Crown District sits atop the highest hill, housing the Royal Palace and the noble estates. The palace itself is a marvel of architecture, with spires that pierce the clouds and walls that have never been breached in the city's 500-year history.

The Merchant Quarter bustles with activity around the Grand Bazaar, where traders from across the known world come to sell their wares. The famous Silverdale Mint produces the kingdom's currency, and the Merchant's Guild maintains its headquarters in a building that rivals the palace in grandeur.

The Harbor District controls the city's lifeblood - trade flowing up and down the rivers. Massive docks can accommodate the largest vessels, and the Harbor Master's tower serves as both a lighthouse and a customs house.

The Scholar's Row houses the Great Library, the Royal Academy, and numerous schools and workshops. The library is said to contain the largest collection of books and scrolls in the known world, including texts from the lost civilization of the First Kingdom.

The Common Quarter provides homes for the working folk, with its famous taverns, craftsmen's shops, and the Fighter's Guild. The district has a warm, community feeling despite being the most populated area of the city.

Silverdale's population swells to over 200,000 during festival seasons, but normally houses about 150,000 residents. The city's magical defenses include a powerful ward that prevents scrying and teleportation within the city limits without proper authorization.
