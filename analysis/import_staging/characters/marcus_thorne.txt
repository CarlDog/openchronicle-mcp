Sir Marcus Thorne is a veteran knight of the Silver Order. At 45 years old, he bears the scars of countless battles but maintains an unwavering sense of honor. His sword, Dawnbreaker, was forged from meteoric steel and glows with a faint blue light. Marcus is known for his dry wit and protective nature toward younger knights.

He stands tall at six feet, with graying brown hair and steel-blue eyes that have seen too much war. A jagged scar runs from his left temple to his jaw, a reminder of the Battle of Crimson Fields. Despite his weathered appearance, Marcus moves with the grace of a seasoned warrior.

Personality-wise, Marcus is pragmatic and direct, preferring action to lengthy discussions. He has a mentor relationship with several young knights and is fiercely loyal to his oath. His greatest fear is failing those under his protection.

Relationships:
- Mentor to Sir Elena Brightshield
- Sworn brother to Captain Aldric Stone
- Serves under Lord Commander Gareth Vale
- Has a complicated past with Lady Evelyn Moonwhisper
