Sir Marcus Thorne - Knight Commander of the Silver Order

Marcus Thorne stands tall at six feet, his weathered face bearing the scars of countless battles. At 45 years old, he has served the Silver Order for over two decades, rising through the ranks through skill, honor, and unwavering dedication. His steel-gray hair is kept short in military fashion, and his piercing blue eyes seem to see through any deception.

Marcus wields Dawnbreaker, a legendary sword forged from meteoric steel that glows with a faint blue light at sunrise and sunset. The blade was passed down from his mentor, Sir Gareth, who died protecting the last heir of the royal bloodline during the War of Broken Crowns.

As Knight Commander, Marcus leads 200 knights and maintains order in the troubled borderlands. He is known for his strategic mind, his protective nature toward younger knights, and his dry sense of humor that helps maintain morale during dark times. Despite his stern exterior, Marcus has a soft spot for orphans and has quietly funded several orphanages throughout the kingdom.

Marcus harbors a secret guilt over his failure to save a village from bandits early in his career, which drives his current obsession with protecting the innocent. He has a complicated relationship with Lady Elara Moonwhisper, the court mage, with whom he shares a history from their younger days.

Key relationships:
- Mentor: Sir Gareth (deceased)
- Romantic interest: Lady Elara Moonwhisper
- Protégé: Sir Kael Brightshield (young knight)
- Rival: Captain Roderick the Bold (ambitious rival within the order)
