[BATTLECHASERS – BESTIARY OF VANYR]

**The world of Vanyr is old and haunted, and her lands remember teeth. Below is a living catalogue of creatures — magical, mythic, and monstrous — that roam the realm. Some are worshipped. Some are hunted. A few… hunt back.**

---

## UNIVERSAL OR CROSS-REGIONAL BEASTS

### **Whisperwights**
- Ethereal remnants of truths never spoken aloud
- Found near ley fractures, libraries, or bedsides during illness
- Feed on secrets; become corporeal when enough are gathered

### **Ashmanes**
- Flame-maned predators descended from drakeblood hounds
- Can burn through lies, tracking by emotional residue
- Hunted in Emberwake, revered in Cindral’s Fall

### **Glassjaws**
- Translucent eel-wyrms found in flooded ruins or mirrors left too long uncovered
- Bite through steel, or memories

### **Starfallen**
- Elemental remnants of celestial impacts
- Each carries a shard of thought too alien to interpret
- Touching one often changes you. Permanently.

### **The Witherkin**
- Forest-warped humanoids infected by arcane blight
- Speak in borrowed voices
- Believed to be the origin of certain fey bloodlines

---

## AELRETH CREATURES

### **Hollowhart Stags**
- Ghost-antlered beasts who lead travelers astray
- Said to be the reincarnations of oathbreakers

### **Lantern Owls**
- Birds whose eyes glow like oil lamps
- Cry sounds like a whisper through leaves
- Symbol of lost nobility

### **Covenboars**
- Aggressive, tusked familiars of wild witches
- Can smell intentions
- Often explode into birds upon death

---

## OLORIN CREATURES

### **Mirrormaws**
- Mask-faced mimic beasts
- Steal voices to replicate appearance and scent
- Feed on unresolved guilt

### **Inkdrakes**
- Small, intelligent wyrms who live in books and scrolls
- Rewrite passages to alter fate subtly
- Sometimes offer deals

### **Cicatrix**
- Humanoid scars given form
- Spawn from failed rituals
- Harbingers of prophecy collapse

---

## EMBERWAKE CREATURES

### **Skyrazors**
- Winged reptiles that nest in lightning fields
- Their cries disrupt magic
- Hunted by Wyrmguard for sport and armor

### **Ashwights**
- Undead remnants of oathbound flamebearers
- Glow with blue fire
- Can only be laid to rest by forgiveness or fire

### **Volclings**
- Lava-born sprites with shifting forms
- Mischievous but capable of mass destruction
- Worshipped by some Ashbinders as fire spirits

---

## VEYRA ISLES CREATURES

### **Deepcallers**
- Leviathan offspring who sing storms
- Sleep for centuries, wake during eclipses
- Often mistaken for islands

### **Tideblight Sirens**
- Not seductive — *contagious*
- Infect the mind with oceanic obsession
- Often follow Driftwake

### **Saltgrims**
- Coral-encrusted drowned undead
- Bound to specific promises or curses
- Cannot cross fresh water

---

## CINDRAL’S FALL CREATURES

### **Crownlurks**
- Crown-shaped parasites that attach to heads
- Grant charisma, ambition — and hallucinations of glory
- Favored by fallen nobles

### **Relictors**
- Animated statues powered by nationalistic memory
- Only attack those who disrespect the empire

### **Vaultspawn**
- Result of broken oaths sealed in tombs
- Born of guilt, armored in ancestral gold

---

## THE REACH BELOW CREATURES

### **Gravemyces**
- Fungus-colony organisms that mimic humanoid shape
- Share one mind, many voices
- Prefer the recently deceased

### **Shiverworms**
- Bioluminescent serpents that whisper futures
- Must be swallowed alive to gain the vision

### **The Latchling**
- Sentient creature disguised as a child
- Sells maps to places that shouldn’t exist
- Eats lies. Also fingers.

---

## NAMED AND LEGENDARY ENTITIES

### **Lilith, The Dark Queen**
- Soulbound entity of seduction, shadow, and war
- Capable of reshaping flesh and thought
- Feared by gods, courted by monsters

### **The Shatterwolf**
- Made of broken weapons and frozen winds
- Roams between borders, howls at injustice
- Only appears to those carrying both grief and hope

### **Mother Below**
- Vast root-goddess that sleeps beneath the Reach
- Dreams cities into ruin
- Worshipped in Veilroot by echo-bonded cults

### **Thornwing Sovereign**
- Last of the dragon-kin with command of sky, flame, and memory
- Said to be hiding as a mortal knight

---

## CREATURE CLASSIFICATIONS

- **Behemoth** – Massive and rare (e.g., Deepcaller, Shatterwolf)
- **Mythkin** – Magical humanoid or semi-intelligent (e.g., Witherkin, Inkdrakes)
- **Feybound** – Originating from Wild Magic or broken rituals (e.g., Hollowhart, Cicatrix)
- **Echoborne** – Memory, sound, or dream-formed (e.g., Whisperwights, Gravemyces)
- **Relicborne** – Created or sustained by history (e.g., Relictors, Vaultspawn)
