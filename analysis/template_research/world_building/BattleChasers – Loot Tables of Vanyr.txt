[BATTLECHASERS – LOOT TABLES OF VANYR]

**Legend:**  
(C) – Common item  
(U) – Uncommon item  
(R) – Rare item  
(X) – Unique or story-defining item



**Loot tables are divided by rarity, region, and source. All items should serve narrative, economic, or magical functions within the world of Vanyr.**

---

## UNIVERSAL LOOT TABLE

### COMMON (C)
- Cracked sigil coin (minor noble house, defunct)
- Whispersteel dagger (non-magical but symbolic)
- Minor potion (restorative, clarity, shadow sight)
- Spell-frayed parchment (retains faint glow)
- Bone dice (haunted if rolled thrice in one hour)

### UNCOMMON (U)
- Emberstone shard (warm to the touch, inert magic)
- Bottle of bottled memory (someone else’s regret)
- Weathered relic mask (from forgotten festival)
- Chalk of warding (5 charges)
- Blood-forged ring (grows warmer when near betrayal)

### RARE (R)
- Sigil-bonded compass (points to your last lie)
- Crying mirror (shows who is missing you)
- Mycorrh-threaded robes (fungal symbiosis, heals slowly)
- Soul-forged coin (usable once in negotiation with a dead thing)
- Fireglass eye (sees through illusions, hates liars)

### UNIQUE (X)
- Key of Unmaking (opens doors that shouldn’t exist)
- Starbrand salve (applies a celestial mark for 7 days)
- Shatterwolf Fang (summons an echo of defiance)
- Lilith’s Breath (one-use vial of her favor or curse — indistinguishable)
