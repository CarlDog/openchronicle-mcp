
[BATTLECHASERS – KNIGHTLY ORDERS]

——————————
AELRETH
——————————
**The Dawnwardens** – Traditional knights of honor and light, though many now serve as ceremonial protectors. Still deadly when roused.  
**Order of the Verdant Blade** – Woodland defenders who wield druidic magic alongside steel. Mounts include elk, stags, and the occasional bear.  
**Knights of the Brass Gate** – Protectors of merchant caravans and city gates. Known less for gallantry and more for ledger enforcement.

——————————
FROSTFELL
——————————
**The Bound Flame** – Elite fire-sworn warriors trained to bind ancient terrors in glacial tombs. Their oaths are tattoos that burn with betrayal.  
**Icewake Guard** – Mountain rangers and siege specialists who wear carved frost-plates. They answer only to old kings and colder gods.  
**Wardens of the Black Drift** – Snow-silent scouts who guard the veil between realms. Ride dire wolves and speak only in wind-code.

——————————
OLORIN
——————————
**Knights Penumbra** – Arcane-touched warriors whose blades hum with stored spells. Code is built on paradox. Their duels are poetic — and final.  
**The Pale Sigil** – Enforcers of magical law, now fractured into mercenary remnants. Each member carries a spell-sealed mask.  
**Order of the Hollow Lance** – Ghost-bonded knights who ride spectral beasts. Their charges are unknown. Their judgment is final.

——————————
KHARAG DÛM
——————————
**Blades of Ash** – Burned initiates wielding slag-forged weapons. Each carries a personal fire — literal and metaphysical.  
**The Black Reclaimers** – Scavenger-knights who unearth cursed relics and bury them again — with the wielders, if necessary.  
**The Scorched Vow** – Fanatics who believe war cleanses corruption. Once you join, you burn your name.

——————————
VEYRA ISLES
——————————
**Tideward Knights** – Coastal defenders who bind armor with coral and oath-pearls. Answer to the Tidebound Accord.  
**Wavecut Blades** – Nomadic sea-duelists. Tradition demands each defeat is marked by a notch carved in the helm.  
**Stormbound Cavaliers** – Air-riders with wind-touched lances. Said to court lightning and fly only during tempests.

——————————
ISLES OF REVERIE / EMBERWAKE
——————————
**Order of the Ember Sigil** – Fire-walkers from Emberwake. Their armor smolders, their vows are branded.  
**Knights of the Whispering Dream** – Reverie guardians clad in veils and mirrored steel. They ride dream-beasts and speak forgotten names.
