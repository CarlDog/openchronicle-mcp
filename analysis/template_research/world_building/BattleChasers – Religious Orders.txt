
[BATTLECHASERS – RELIGIOUS ORDERS]

——————————
AELRETH
——————————
**Order of the Quiet Flame** – Teaches that warmth and fire are both creation and destruction. Their priests are gentle until they are not.  
**Children of the Grove** – Forest mystics who worship the memory of trees and speak to saplings like ancestors.  
**The Bellmother’s Choir** – Monastic singers whose chants are said to calm spirits and storms. All are mute outside ritual.

——————————
FROSTFELL
——————————
**Forge-Saints** – Revere the divine act of crafting. Their temples are forges, and their prayers are sparks.  
**Brothers of Icewake** – Cold ascetics who believe suffering unlocks enlightenment. Most don’t last a season.  
**The Everthrone** – Worship a god said to slumber beneath the glacier. Followers leave offerings of heat, hoping never to wake it.

——————————
OLORIN
——————————
**The Hollow Chant** – Silent congregation. Their prayers are written on skin. They believe words drain magic from the soul.  
**Eyes of the Veil** – Oracular order devoted to glimpsing hidden truths. Each member binds their sight in cloth and ink.  
**The Wax Saints** – Statues melted during rituals to “release divine essence.” The wax often moves. Sometimes it speaks.

——————————
KHARAG DÛM
——————————
**Flameborn Ascendants** – Worship fire as both origin and judgment. Their relics are brands.  
**Scorch Temple** – Wandering zealots who ignite sacred texts to “free the word.”  
**Whispers of Ash** – Believe life is only real in its last moments. Their sermons are funerals for the living.

——————————
VEYRA ISLES
——————————
**Tide-Mothers** – Sea priestesses who anoint the newborn with saltwater and whisper their fate to the waves.  
**Deepcallers** – Interpret whale songs and deep trench murmurs as divine edicts. Build cathedrals from driftwood and bone.  
**Drowned Keepers** – Protectors of sunken temples. They drown in ritual, then walk again.

——————————
ISLES OF REVERIE / EMBERWAKE
——————————
**Ashen Spiral** – Emberwake order of pain-walkers who believe transformation lies in surviving the flame.  
**Dreamwell Covenant** – Meditative order in Reverie that records dreams as prophecy. Their temples are libraries of memory.
