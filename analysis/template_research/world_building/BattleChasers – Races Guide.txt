
[BATTLECHASERS – GUIDE TO PLAYABLE & MAJOR RACES]

The world of BattleChasers features a diverse range of humanoid and magical species, each with their own cultures, powers, and quirks.

——————————
HUMAN
——————————
Adaptable and widespread. Humans build cities, empires, and cults in equal measure. Ambition is both their greatest gift and curse.

——————————
ELF
——————————
Long-lived and often aloof, elves come in various bloodlines: high elves (arcane scholars), wood elves (forest-bound and primal), and dusk elves (secretive and pale-eyed). Magic seeps into their bones.

——————————
HALF-ELF
——————————
Born of two worlds, half-elves are natural diplomats, mercenaries, and wanderers. They’re welcome everywhere—and truly at home nowhere.

——————————
DWARF
——————————
Stout, honor-bound, and stubborn. Mountain dwarves dominate the forges of Frostfell, while forest dwarves of Aelreth practice woodcraft and earth magic.

——————————
GNOME
——————————
Inventive, chaotic, and joyfully nosy. Glimmergnomes in particular tend to tinker with magic like it’s a toy—often with explosive results.

——————————
TIEFLING
——————————
Mortals touched by infernal legacy or ancient pacts. Their horns, tails, and glowing eyes mark them—though their destinies remain their own.

——————————
DRAGONBORN
——————————
Descendants of ancient dragons or born of volcanic rites. Most reside in Kharag Dûm and treat fire as both bloodline and religion.

——————————
ORC / ASH ORC
——————————
Ash Orcs of the Iron Wastes are hardened by volcanic storms and brutal creeds. Tribal, loyal, and deadly when roused.

——————————
GOLEM-KIN
——————————
Living constructs granted sentience. Rare and revered—or feared. Some serve as silent protectors, others seek to craft their own soul.

——————————
SHIFTER
——————————
Shapechangers tied to the sea or the moon. Common in the Veyra Isles. Unpredictable, instinctive, and loyal to their own.

——————————
SEA ELF
——————————
Aquatic elves adapted to the depths. Graceful and sharp-eyed. Their songs shape currents and influence sea creatures.

——————————
GOLIATH
——————————
Massive humanoids from Frostfell’s high ranges. Stoic, blunt, and driven by trials of strength and legacy.

——————————
HALFLING
——————————
Smaller than humans, larger than gnomes. Known for luck, food, and surviving situations far above their weight class.

——————————
KOBOLD
——————————
Draconic tinkers and cave-dwellers. Often underestimated, usually to the underestimator’s regret.

——————————
OTHER (RARE OR UNCOMMON)
——————————
- Selkies: Oceanic shapeshifters tied to seal-skins  
- Starborne: Mortals marked by celestial prophecy  
- Fey-Blooded: Touched by dream realms and moonlight  
- Undying: Rare necromantic revenants with lingering purpose  
- Emberborn: Individuals infused with elemental flame (Emberwake origin)
