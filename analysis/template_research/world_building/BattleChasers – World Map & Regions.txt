
[WORLD MAP – BATTLECHASERS]

The world of BattleChasers spans five major regions and two notable island chains. Trade, war, and prophecy bind these lands — each with its own mystery, history, and voice.

———————————————
MAINLAND REGIONS
———————————————

**Aelreth – The Emerald Reach**  
Temperate forests, river valleys, old ruins, and sprawling farmlands.  
- Notable Cities: Thornevale (Karl’s hometown), Calharra, Stillmarch  
- Ports: Calharra (to Veyra Isles), Mistway Harbor  
- Predominant Races: Humans, Half-elves, Forest Dwarves  
- Factions: Trader’s Guild, Veilwatch (ancient druidic wardens), House Thalen (nobility)

**Frostfell – The North Holds**  
Tundra and glacier peaks, home to warrior clans and ancient frost magic.  
- Notable Cities: Brakholm Deep, Ironthrone, Vargal’s Rest  
- Ports: Vargal’s Rest (to Veyra Isles and Olorin)  
- Predominant Races: Dwarves, Goliaths, Iceblood Orcs  
- Factions: Stonekin Brotherhood, Forge-Saints, Order of the Bound Flame

**Cindral’s Fall – The Broken Empire**
Once the seat of imperial glory, now fractured across cities of ruin, pride, and undeath.
- Notable Cities: Halcyn (ruined capital), Gravemount (necropolis-metropolis), Streyhold (military frontier)
- Ports: Limited inland river access via the Mournway Canal
- Predominant Races: Humans, Tieflings, Undying, Golem-kin
- Factions: Shardborne Houses, Boneledger Court, Iron Assembly, Order of the Last Mantle

**Olorin – The Shattered Dominion**  
Broken kingdoms ruled by magic and madness.  
- Notable Cities: Virelock, Whisperhollow, Sablegate  
- Ports: Sablegate (to Veyra Isles)  
- Predominant Races: Elves, Tieflings, Sorcerous bloodlines  
- Factions: The Hollow Court (arcane elite), Red Veil (cult of silence), Starborne Seers

**Kharag Dûm – The Iron Waste**  
A desert of shattered stone and buried gods, riddled with tombs and smoke-filled skies.  
- Notable Cities: Ashforge, Redspire, Scarhold  
- Ports: None (landlocked)  
- Predominant Races: Dragonborn, Ash Orcs, Nomadic Humans  
- Factions: Ember Pact, Scorchfang Raiders, Seekers of the Hollow Star

**The Veyra Isles – The Wandering Chain**  
Archipelago with unpredictable currents and magical storms.  
- Notable Cities: Everharbor, Hightide, Myrrhold  
- Ports: All cities connected by ship; reach Frostfell, Olorin, and Aelreth  
- Predominant Races: Sea Elves, Selkies, Shifters  
- Factions: Tidebound Accord, Order of the Deep, Stormsingers’ Guild

———————————————
ISLAND CHAINS
———————————————

**Isle of Reverie** – A mist-shrouded island rumored to house the ruins of a forgotten civilization.  
- Access: Reachable only by moon-tide routes from Everharbor  
- Factions: None known, but cursed magic lingers

**Emberwake Spires** – Jagged volcanic islands pulsing with unstable magic and hidden drakes.  
- Access: Treacherous journey from Redspire via air or enchanted vessel  
- Factions: Rogue elementalists and draconic cults

———————————————
REGIONAL CONNECTIONS
———————————————
- Aelreth shares a southern border with Frostfell and a western border with Kharag Dûm  
- Olorin lies southeast of Aelreth, accessible by land or sea  
- The Veyra Isles sit to the west of Aelreth and can be reached from Frostfell, Olorin, or Calharra  
- Kharag Dûm connects to Aelreth via the Deadstone Pass
