
[BATTLECHASERS – GUILDS & FACTIONS]

——————————
AELRETH
——————————
**Trader’s Guild** – Oversees regional commerce, shipping, and neutral trade agreements. Ruthless in contracts, generous in bribes.  
**Veilwatch** – Druidic wardens protecting ancient groves and forbidden places. Few speak, fewer ever leave the trees.  
**House Thalen** – Old noble bloodline, now a political force in Stillmarch. Noble only in title — their coin runs thicker than their honor.

——————————
FROSTFELL
——————————
**Stonekin Brotherhood** – A dwarven union of builders, smiths, and warriors who protect heritage above all else.  
**Forge-Saints** – Religious artisans who sanctify weapon and armor crafting. Their forges are sacred. Their tempers are not.  
**Order of the Bound Flame** – Knightly sect sworn to imprison old gods and living storms. Oaths are sealed in fire and blood.

——————————
OLORIN
——————————
**The Hollow Court** – Ruling circle of high mages, mad seers, and cursed aristocrats. Obsession with power disguised as governance.  
**Red Veil** – Cult that worships silence, entropy, and the unraveling of magic. Their faces are masked. Their thoughts... might not be their own.  
**Starborne Seers** – Visionaries who claim to read the skies of other worlds. Occasionally accurate. Always unsettling.

——————————
KHARAG DÛM
——————————
**Ember Pact** – Brotherhood of fire-priests, elementalists, and war-monks. Burn to purify. Burn to remember.  
**Scorchfang Raiders** – Nomadic warbands, part mercenary, part scavenger cult. They believe conquest earns favor from ancient beings.  
**Seekers of the Hollow Star** – Wandering mystics chasing a prophecy none can fully recall. Still, they chase.

——————————
VEYRA ISLES
——————————
**Tidebound Accord** – Naval alliance of privateers, diplomats, and sorcerers. They rule the seas in everything but name.  
**Order of the Deep** – Devotees of sea spirits, leviathans, and the endless trench. Wear inked blessings and speak in waves.  
**Stormsingers’ Guild** – Bards, mages, and pilots who navigate storms with music. Risky. Profound. Addictive.

——————————
ISLES OF REVERIE / EMBERWAKE
——————————
**The Veiled Flame** – Secretive order of flamebound scholars seeking truth through self-immolation and rebirth. Active only in Emberwake.  
**Caretakers of the Dreamwell** – Guardians of a hidden temple in Reverie. They walk in waking dreams, and speak in shared memory.
