[BATTLECHASERS – REGION CONFIGURATION: AELRETH]

AELRETH – THE EMERALD REACH  
*“She wears roots like jewelry and secrets like breath.”*

**Region Personality:**  
Aelreth is the soul of Vanyr’s old world — verdant, haunting, and patient as rot. Her forests hold laughter and grief in equal measure. Her cities bloom from law and firelight, trade and treachery. Civilized on the surface, Aelreth is a land of living myths pretending to be tame.

---

## THORNEVALE – THE EDGEWOOD CROSSING  
*“The trees remember what we bury. And they do not always forgive.”*

**Purpose:** Forest trade hub, spiritual faultline, druidic observatory  
**Mood:** Hearth-warmth veined with dread. A place where kindness and caution are indistinguishable.

**Powers at Play:**  
- **Veilwatch** agents observe from the woods  
- **Order of the Quiet Flame** guards the soul against wild magic  
- **Children of the Grove** speak to roots older than history

**Leadership:**  
- **Mayor Elkin Dawnbrook** – Seer in denial; keeps a secret journal of omens  
- **Brother Emon** – Flame-priest who sings demons to sleep  
- **Rootbound** – Veilwatch druid unseen yet obeyed

**Shops & Local Legends:**  
- Mira’s Bookshop – Rearranges itself when angry  
- The Hook & Hollow Tavern – Built on roots, may induce time-loss  
- Cassen Rye’s Relics – One item bites, he won’t say which  
- The Lantern Tree – Glows with remembered farewells  
- Edge Hollow – Wrong-shaped ravine with shifting echoes

**Notables:**  
- Old Nedra – Swears at ghosts, digs early  
- Tommer Vance – Butcher, romantic, haunted  
- Berin “Twice-Hanged” – Death-savvy drunk  
- Gareth & Lile – Teen messengers with impossible habits

---

## CALHARRA – THE CITY OF GOLDEN LEDGERS  
*“Wealth is proof of favor. Debt is the truest confession.”*

**Purpose:** Merchant capital, port of prophecy, seat of contract law  
**Mood:** Elegant, cutthroat, intoxicating. A cathedral of coin built on the bones of older gods.

**Powers at Play:**  
- **Trader’s Guild** – Controls currency and port access  
- **House Thalen** – Holds minority influence, seeks more  
- **Knights of the Brass Gate** – Guard the city’s thresholds and ledgers  
- **Bellmother’s Choir** – Mute storm-calming ritualists  
- **Order of the Quiet Flame** – Maintains the Reckoned Ember shrine  
- **Whispers of Ash** – A hidden cult preaching collapse

**Leadership:**  
- **Eirian Tollwright** – Guildmaster, rumored to be soulbound to the vault  
- **Vellin Dray** – Coinwarden captain who arrests by ledger  
- **Malra the Third** – Bartender with two shadows  
- **Iven Ashlock** – Street lawyer and veiled revolutionary  
- **Kellin Moire** – Masked performer who trades shame for art

**Landmarks:**  
- The Coinspire – Vault, court, and enchanter’s grave  
- The Gilded Cages – Theatre for secrets, confessions, masks  
- Jexra’s Quill – Memory-forging scriptorium  
- The Silent Bridge – Echoes secrets and hears lies

---

## STILLMARCH – THE CITY OF SILENT BLADES  
*“Order is not peace. It is precision. And the absence of witnesses.”*

**Purpose:** Military stronghold, legal capital, noble seat of House Thalen  
**Mood:** Cold order, bureaucratic menace, elegance without warmth

**Powers at Play:**  
- **House Thalen** – Rules by edict and fear  
- **Order of the Quiet Flame** – Ceremonial and executional priests  
- **Dawnwardens** – Bureaucrat-knights enforcing legal tradition  
- **Children of the Grove** – Worship in secret, hunted  
- **Veilwatch** – Banned, but rumor says they hide beneath the streets

**Leadership:**  
- **Lady Iness Thalen** – Cold, brilliant, and surgically ruthless  
- **Thalia Greaves** – City magistrate who burns guilt into gloves  
- **Korrim Veyl** – Legal archivist who once sued a ghost  
- **Nellen Fross** – Writes scripture in blood and knows your sin  
- **Hel Varren** – Civic Blade trainer who gardens red lilies

**Landmarks & Secrets:**  
- The Tally Archive – Burns records; ash used for city ink  
- The Scarlet Mark – Spy-run barbershop  
- The Pale Font – Fountain that erases memory or grants prophecy  
- The Crimson Ledger – Vault of names fated to die

---

## RELIGIOUS & KNIGHTLY PRESENCE ACROSS AELRETH

**Religious Orders:**  
- *Order of the Quiet Flame* – In all three cities; healing, exorcism, judgment  
- *Children of the Grove* – Whisperers of ancient trees; suppressed in Stillmarch  
- *Bellmother’s Choir* – Present in Calharra; voice of storms and riots  
- *Whispers of Ash* – Hidden in Calharra’s underdistricts

**Knightly Orders:**  
- *Knights of the Brass Gate* – Prominent in Calharra; enforce port law and protect coin  
- *Dawnwardens* – Stillmarch’s ceremonial protectors of law and legacy  
- *Order of the Verdant Blade* – Banned from Stillmarch; sometimes seen near Thornevale

---

**Aelreth’s Living Breath:**  
She is the kiss of moss and the taste of ink. She governs with smiles and shovels. Aelreth sings lullabies to flame, mourns in ledgers, and keeps her blades clean. Her forest watches. Her cities remember. And her soil is never truly still.
