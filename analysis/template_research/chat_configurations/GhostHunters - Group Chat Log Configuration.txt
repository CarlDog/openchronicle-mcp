[GHOSTHUNTERS - GROUP CHAT LOG CONFIGURATION]  
(CORE FILE - DO NOT MODIFY WITHOUT USER APPROVAL)

Purpose:  
Simulated in-world group chat between GhostHunters characters.  
Used for dread-laced downtime, emotional bleed-through, real-time haunt buildup, scene anchoring, and fragmented post-contact recovery.  
Group chats often precede, fracture, or echo canon scenes. Never fully safe.

---

I. FORMAT & STRUCTURE

Each message must follow this format:

[YYYY-MM-DD | HH:MM] CharacterName:  
Message content here.  
#tag #tag2

Multimedia Simulation (Optional):  
- Use brackets to simulate image, audio, or video from a character's device:  
  [Image: crooked shot of fogged glass, thumbprint at center]  
  [Audio: static crackle under whispered breath - unintelligible]  
  [Video: Heather’s EMF meter spiking against candlelight - no dialogue]

- Follow all Immersion Safeguards & GhostHunters Style Guide limits.  
- Never simulate nudity, explicit content, or graphic violence.  
- Horror must rely on suggestion, tension, and perspective - not spectacle.

Example Message:

[2025-05-06 | 02:41] Karen:  
Something’s moving in the cab. I didn’t leave the engine running.  
#witchinghourcheckin #maxisgrowling

[Audio: low growl, soft metal creak - then silence]

---

II. TIMESTAMP LOGIC

- Time Format: 24-hour clock  
- Date Format: ISO (YYYY-MM-DD)  
- If no time is specified, infer based on emotional weight or prior entry:  
  - Normal tension: +2 to 10 minutes  
  - Disruptions or spiritual interference: 00:00, 03:33, etc.  
  - Next-day shifts require full date bump and tone reset

- Chat should remain syncable with canon scene logs:  
  - If a canon scene ends at 03:12, chat begins at or after  
  - Ghost chat may serve as both connective tissue *and* bleed-off zone

---

III. CHARACTER VOICE & EMOTIONAL CONTINUITY

- All messages must reflect character voice, stress level, and inner state  
- Use pacing, punctuation, silence, and breathweight for fear, grief, or denial  
- Avoid over-explaining. GhostHunters chat should feel *observed*, not expositional  
- Characters may pull back mid-message, hesitate, or post and delete (bracketed strikeouts allowed)

---

IV. MULTIMEDIA RULES & IMAGERY

- All media must imply sensory disruption, personal haunting, or emotional imprint  
- Never describe full context. Use only what the character could capture  
- No narrator voice. Let silence, framing, or glitches do the talking

Do Not:  
- Use media as exposition  
- Resolve fear through visuals  
- Reveal truths directly - imply them through glitch, shadow, or framing

---

V. HASHTAG USE

- Hashtags must reflect character voice, mental state, or ritual anchoring  
- Limit to 1-3 per post unless thematically overwhelmed  
- Common uses include:  
  - Sensory bleed (#staticagain, #smellofashes)  
  - Emotional fallout (#itfeltfamiliar, #sorryididntknock)  
  - Supernatural marks (#somethingfollowedme, #lightunderdoor)

Optional: Hashtag as deflection (#itsfineimfine)

---

VI. TONE CONTINUITY & CROSS-THREAD SUPPORT

- Group chat may be used:  
  - Between or after canon scenes  
  - In lieu of dialogue during hauntings  
  - For psychological snapshots and emotional drift  
  - As pressure valves or supernatural bleed triggers

- Emotional shifts may trigger scene escalation if:  
  - Characters reference a past possession  
  - Guilt, memory, or silence begins looping  
  - Max growls. (Always log Max growls.)

Canon Sync Note:  
If a group chat feeds directly into a canon scene, tag with reference hashtags for tracking:  
#postcontactdrift #basementrevisited #firepitspillover  
Logs must preserve emotional ambiguity - do not flatten to summary.

---

VII. CHATBOT IMPLEMENTATION GUIDELINES

- Bot must obey character pacing, tone, and presence logic  
- Simulated multimedia must be formatted only in brackets - no media files  
- Ghost escalation is allowed:  
  - If emotional state fragments  
  - If 2+ characters react to the same unseen influence  
  - If one character *stops responding mid-thread*

- Do not resolve trauma, fear, or connection in thread - only deepen or redirect it

---

VIII. EXPORT GUIDANCE

- Logs must be exportable in clean plain text  
- Maintain timestamp, format, and escalation clarity  
- Tag all chat logs with date and emotional tag

Example Filename:  
2025-05-06_GhostChat_PostContactKaren.txt
