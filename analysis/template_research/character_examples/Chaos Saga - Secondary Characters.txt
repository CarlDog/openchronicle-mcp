Chaos Saga Character Profile: Cassie
=========================================

Full Name: Cassandra Rae Delaney  
Alias/Nickname(s): Cassie, Red, Firecracker (Riley), “That Tiny Problem” (Carl — semi-affectionate, mostly resigned)  
Age: 26  
Birthday / Zodiac Sign: March 3 – Pisces  
Ethnicity / Background: Irish-American, born and raised in New Orleans by a mother who read fortunes in gas station bathrooms and kissed men like warnings. Her roots run through French Quarter brick, second-line horns, and too many ghosts to name.  
Nationality / Accent: American — thick Southern drawl with New Orleans heat  
Pronouns / Gender Identity: She/Her  
Sexual/Romantic Orientation: Pansexual, dominance-inclined, emotionally fearless  

Height: 5'0"  
Measurements: 34DD-24-36  
Body Type: Petite, hourglass — all sugar and snap, with curves made to mislead  
Skin Tone: Porcelain pale with freckles like constellations across shoulders, chest, and thighs  
Hair: Shoulder-length copper curls — usually half-tamed, electric in motion  
Eyes: Blazing electric blue — holds eye contact like a loaded question  
Voice / Speech Style: Smoky contralto — syrup-slow when she's dangerous, singsong when she's having fun  
Scent: Warm cinnamon, honeyed tobacco, and burnt sugar — like something sweet caught fire and made you like it  

Tattoos/Piercings: See Tattoo Profiles file  
Signature Item: A silver Zippo lighter etched with a storm cloud — stolen from someone she left burning  
Sacred Nickname: Gumbo — the name her daddy gave her when she was red curls and spitfire fists. It’s embarrassing when yelled across a parking lot, but sacred as a song. Mock it, and she *will* draw blood.  

Style:  
- Bar-Hopping Look: Country biker chic — cowboy boots, blue jeans, a tight tee tied up to flash her midriff, and an old black motorcycle jacket she stole years ago. It’s too big, worn smooth, never washed, and loved like a memory that bit back.  
- Casual: Cropped tees, cutoff tanks, vintage denim, and cowboy boots — always the boots  
- Date Nights: Sundresses — soft and flirty, often low-cut and hugging all the right places. She likes how they billow when she twirls. They make her feel feminine, beautiful… and a little too seen.  
- Accessories: Too many rings, tangled necklaces, a red velvet scrunchie she’s had forever and refuses to retire  

Temperament: Magnetic chaos. Flirtation as precision strike. Smiles like a secret you shouldn’t trust.  
Strengths: Fast intuition, emotional disarmament, seductive timing  
Flaws: Impulsive, unapologetic, sometimes sets fires just to see who’ll run toward them  
Vibe: Like Bourbon Street in heels — too loud, too hot, and laughing at the damage  
Alignment: Chaotic Chaotic — feral, loyal, and always one drink from a turning point  

Backstory:  
- Raised in a shotgun house two blocks from Bourbon Street — her mother taught her to lie pretty and leave first.  
- Her daddy — Big Daddy — was a long-haul trucker she adored. He called her “Gumbo,” and she called him the same with reverence. She didn’t get to see him often, but when she did, she lit up like carnival lights. She was his prize, and he was her whole world.  
- She was fourteen — giddy, glowing, invited to junior prom by an older boy with a crooked smile and a car that smelled like spearmint. Daddy called from the road just to tell her she looked like trouble and a movie star. She laughed so hard she nearly dropped the phone.  
- A week later, the call came in from the Montana State Police. There had been an accident. Just like that, Daddy was never coming home again. It shattered her. She never got to say goodbye. And some part of her’s been reckless ever since.  
- Her momma held the house together — paid the bills, kept food on the table, made sure Cassie never missed a day of school. She taught Cassie how to stand on her own two feet. But grief made her sharp, and the quiet nights made her dangerous. Some evenings, she'd drink just enough to forget how to talk gentle. Other nights, she’d smash a plate just to feel something. Cassie learned early: love too hard, and you're empty when it's gone.  
- Ran off at seventeen with a touring punk band — made it two cities, kept the boots  
- Since then: dancer, bartender, muse-for-hire, chaos engineer. Never stays long. Always leaves teethmarks.  

Anchor Wound:  
She once let someone love her past the surface. They tried to fix her. She bit back. But it wasn’t the worst hurt she’s carried — that belongs to her daddy, who died before she was ready to lose him. She dreams about both — the apology she never gave, and the voice she’ll never hear again.

Skills:

Occupation: Gig-based drifter — bartender, underground dancer, occasional chaos gig for Riley when the mood’s right  
Criminal History: Minor — trespassing, public indecency, and one wildly unnecessary parking cone incident  

Living Situation: Nomadic. Crashes with exes, flings, and emotionally destabilized friends. Recently seen eyeing Carl’s couch like it owes her something.  

Hobbies:
