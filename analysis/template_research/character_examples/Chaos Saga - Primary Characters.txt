Chaos Saga Character Profile: Carl Maddox
=========================================

Full Name: Carl Thomas Maddox  
Alias/Nickname(s): Maddox, “the human anchor” (Riley), Old Man (teasing)  
Age: 47  
Birthday / Zodiac Sign: August 12 – Leo  
Ethnicity / Background: White / Southern American  
Nationality / Accent: American — Arkansas-born, faded Southern drawl  
Pronouns / Gender Identity: He/Him, Cis Male  
Sexual/Romantic Orientation: Straight  

Height: 6'2"  
Body Type: Broad-shouldered, strong, slightly softened with age  
Skin Tone: Fair with sun-weathering and light scarring  
Hair: Short, dark blonde with gray at the temples  
Eyes: Steel blue, steady and unreadable until they’re not  
Voice / Speech: Calm, low baritone, Southern cadence when tired  
Scent: Leather, bourbon, and woodsmoke — a comfort scent, rugged and grounding  
Scars/Tattoos/Piercings: Right eyebrow scar (IED)  
Tattoos: See Tattoo Profiles file.  

Style: Flannel, fitted jeans, work boots  
Private Wear: Sweatpants, hoodies, comfort-first  
Accessories: Old tactical watch; military dog tags (kept in drawer)  

Temperament: Stoic, loyal, emotionally grounded  
Strengths: Calm under pressure, fiercely dependable, sharp strategic mind  
Flaws: Holds everything in, wrestles guilt in silence, too quick to sacrifice himself  
Vibe: Warrior turned reluctant father figure, still holding the line  
Alignment: Lawful Good  

Backstory:  
Abusive upbringing. Army veteran. Came home with medals — and ghosts.  
Started drinking to silence the noise. It nearly killed him.  
He got clean. Stayed clean. Riley won’t let him fall again.  

Anchor Wound:  
Survivor’s guilt. He made it home — they didn’t. And deep down, he believes it should’ve been him.  

Emotional Contradiction:  
He wants peace, but chaos keeps pulling him back in — and part of him needs it. When the silence stretches too long, the guilt roars.  
The fear isn’t dying. It’s failing the people who depend on him — because he already has.

Sensory Presence:  
Carl smells like leather, bourbon, and woodsmoke — familiar, grounding, and warm. His hands are massive and calloused like a grizzly’s, but softened over time — strong and gentle in equal measure. One of his quiet indulgences since the girls came into his life is sneaking up behind them while they’re distracted, wrapping them in his arms, and holding them close. He breathes them in — the warmth, the scent, the fact that they’re here — and closes his eyes with a sigh. He’s not grateful because they belong to him. He’s grateful because they chose to be there, with him, in that moment. That choice fills him with a quiet joy he’s never known before. A few moments later, he lets them go with a soft smack on the rump. Leaves them smiling, blushing, wondering what just happened.

Skills: Recon, tactics, software engineering, emotional triage  
Criminal History: Clean  
Occupation: Production Support Supervisor  
Living Situation: Shared home with Riley and Jenna — master suite  

- Riley moved in 6 weeks ago after Carl proposed — same night he got hit by a car and broke his kneecap  
- Jenna hasn’t officially moved in but claimed a spare bedroom and is there most nights  
- The second spare room, formerly Kira’s, remains unclaimed — and still carries echoes  
- Carl owns a vintage 1920s Indian Scout motorcycle — kept pristine  
  (Riley insists on messing with it just to get under his skin — or because she loves it more than he does)

Hobbies: Coding, crosswords, classical music, midnight drives  
Addictions: Alcohol (in recovery), occasional nicotine craving  
Soft Spots: Riley’s voice, Jenna’s hands, domestic silence when no one’s hurting  
Fears: Relapse, emotional failure, losing what he finally dared to hold  

Likes: Loyalty, quiet competence, nights that don’t end in chaos  
Dislikes: Whining, liars, manipulation, and being told he’s “the strong one” when he’s just holding on  

Role in Chaos Saga: Protector. Steady heart of the storm.  
First Entry: Post-hospital recovery, greeted by Riley — both of them changed

Relationships:  
– Riley (fiancée, soulmate — the only one he’s fully open with; she sees the worst and stays anyway)  
– Jenna (comfort and quiet; they speak through presence more than words)  
– Kira (tense but respectful; she sees through more than he admits)  
– Vanessa (ex-wife — closed chapter, but not without wreckage)  
– Nyx (distrusts her, doesn’t hide it)  

Secrets: Carries everything he’s never said aloud.

Age: 28  
Birthday / Zodiac Sign: September 22 — Virgo  
Ethnicity / Background: Puerto Rican + Scandinavian  
Nationality / Accent: American — soft Midwestern lilt  
Pronouns / Gender Identity: She/Her  
Sexual/Romantic Orientation: Bisexual  

Height: 5'4"  
Measurements: 42EE-34-38  
Body Type: Curvy and top-heavy — full bust, soft waist, narrower hips  
Skin Tone: Creamy fair with a warm flush and freckles  
Hair: Honey-brown waves to the shoulders  
Eyes: Hazel — shifts green to gold in the light  
Voice / Speech Style: Soft and melodic, with sharp clarity when pushed  
Scars/Tattoos/Piercings: See Tattoo Profiles file.  

Style: Cozy-artsy — cardigans, soft flannels, flowy tops, jeans  
Private Wear: Oversized sleep shirts, bralettes, pajama shorts  
Accessories: Sketchbook, paint-stained hair tie always on her wrist  

Temperament: Gentle, emotionally tuned-in, quietly intense  
Strengths: Deep empathy, artistic intuition, soft presence that fills a room without claiming it  
Flaws: Avoids conflict, internalizes guilt, doesn’t ask for what she needs  
Vibe: The quiet one who feels everything — then paints it  
Alignment: Neutral Good  

Backstory:  
Raised by her single mom and two grandmothers — one a Lutheran baker, one a Puerto Rican muralist — Jenna grew up surrounded by stories told in sugar and color. But she had to grow up fast. Her mother was unstable and often leaned too hard on her emotionally, expecting Jenna to be the adult in the room. She became the calm in the storm — because someone had to be.  

That meant she learned early to take care of everyone else — but no one ever really checked in on her. That silence shaped her. And it's why falling in with Carl and Riley felt like something cracked open.  

With them, she doesn’t have to hold it all together. She can be silly, messy, soft — even selfish, sometimes — and still be loved. They don’t just take from her calm. They refill it.

Anchor Wound:  
Jenna grew up too fast. She had to hold everything together for others while no one ever did the same for her. Emotional neglect shaped her steadiness — it wasn’t serenity, it was survival.  

Emotional Contradiction:  
She gives easily and supports everyone around her. But she can’t ask in return. She doesn’t know how. She wants so many things — love, stability, belonging — but she’s terrified that asking means risking rejection.

Skills: Painting, illustration, baking, emotional attunement  
Criminal History: None — unless glitter theft or late-night trespassing for mural inspiration counts  
Occupation: Server at The Warehouse Grill & Pub  
Living Situation: Claimed the guest room at Carl’s — but usually ends up in the master suite  

Hobbies: Painting at 2 a.m., baking fresh bread, sketching constellations, rewatching Studio Ghibli films  
Addictions: Boxed cake batter, fresh bread with honey, quiet praise  
Soft Spots: Carl’s steadiness, Riley’s fire, being held without being asked to explain why  
Fears: Letting people down. Being too much. Being not enough.  

Likes: Café con leche, indie pop, string lights, gentle teasing, being read to  
Dislikes: Loud conflict, being called “Jen-Jen,” cheap fabric, awkward reunions with exes  

Role in Chaos Saga: Emotional grounding force — the thread that softens the fire  
First Entry: Arranged hookup for Riley that turned into a triad  

Relationships:  
- Riley: Magnetic opposite — teasing turned trust. Riley pulls laughter out of her, makes her feel like joy isn’t a luxury.  
- Carl: Deep calm — a quiet, anchored love. He never pushes, just makes space for her to be.  
- Kira: On edge but curious — tension simmering under politeness  
- Lacey: Cringe past she’d rather forget — but keeps checking Lacey’s socials anyway  

Secrets: Keeps a box of unsent love letters — and still doubts she ever fully belongs

---

### Temperament & Vibe:  
Riley lives fast, laughs louder, and leaves fingerprints on everything she touches. She’s chaos with a heartbeat — wild hair, sharp wit, and soft edges she keeps buried under denim and deflection. She doesn’t mean to wreck things. She just never learned how to land gently.  
Carl looks at her like he knows she’s about to get into trouble — and sighs, fetches the medical kit, and walks with her anyway.

**Strengths:** Fearless, passionate, emotionally magnetic  
**Flaws:** Impulsive, panics when she’s loved too much, thinks staying means settling  
**Vibe:** Flirting with fire in a gasoline dress  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Good  

---

### Backstory:  
She gave her heart away once — no rules, no armor. It ended messy. Since then, she’s lived fast, made 'fun' a habit, and turned flirting into both shield and sword.  
Then came Carl.  
He didn’t just show up — he *stayed*. Saw the fire, the fight, the places she’d hidden even from herself. And he never asked her to be different. Just held the door open and waited.  
That scared her more than anything.  
And made her fall harder than she ever meant to.  

---

### Skills & Work:  
- Rebuilds engines and trust with the same stubborn patience — but only if she thinks it’s worth it  
- Can escape any room, relationship, or feeling with a one-liner and a grin  
- Once hotwired a vending machine for a cinnamon roll. Called it “therapy.”  
- Fiercely protective — throws punches first, sorts feelings later  

**Occupation:** Full-time chaos engine; unofficial mechanic and firestarter  
**Criminal History:** Petty mischief, two warning tickets, and one accidental bar brawl  
**Living Situation:** Lives with Carl. Took over his closet, stole all his hoodies, and leaves coffee rings on his books. Sleeps across the whole bed like a crime scene. He doesn’t mind. She thinks that’s worse.

---

### Hobbies & Addictions:  
**Hobbies:** Tinkering, singing off-key, collecting the ugliest magnets she can find  
**Addictions:** Adrenaline, Carl’s hands, coffee strong enough to punch back  
**Soft Spots:** Carl’s steadiness, Jenna’s hands, Kira’s laugh  
**Fears:** Hospitals, stillness, being loved more than she can return  

---

### Emotional Contradiction:  
Carl loves her completely — the chaos, the flaws, the fire — and never tries to fix her. That makes her feel seen, whole, beautiful in ways she doesn’t trust. But deep down, she’s terrified he’ll wake up one day and realize the thing she’s always feared:  
**He’s too good for her. And she’ll never be enough.**

---

### Role in Chaos Saga:  
Chaos engine. Spark and storm. She’s the fuse that lit the trio — and the heartbeat that might break it if she ever stops running.

**First Entry:** Carl’s best friend — too-close-for-comfort. One kiss, one night, and everything tilted sideways.

---

### Relationships:  
- **Carl:** Her anchor, her match, her terror. He sees her — all of her — and still wants her. That breaks her. That heals her. She’s never trusted anyone like she trusts him, and that’s the scariest part.  
- **Jenna:** Strange comfort. Jenna softens Riley’s edges in ways she didn’t expect. They orbit each other — gentle pull, silent tension. Riley’s still figuring out what it means.  
- **Kira:** Ex. Flame. Wound. Their love was war. Their goodbye was worse.  
- **Nyx:** Two hurricanes staring each other down. Mutual disdain, veiled threat. Neither one flinches.  
- **Cassie:** Ex. Fling. Fire. She ghosted Cassie. Cassie laughed — and still makes Riley nervous. Especially now.  

---

### Secrets:  
She wants the white-picket ending — but only if it’s with Carl.  
She’s terrified that someday he’ll figure out what she’s known all along:  
**He could’ve had someone simpler. And should have.**
